Buttons

Versatile buttons with variants, sizes, states & interactive effects.

3 Styles
Variants & Styles
HTML 
<!-- Solid Variants -->
<button class="btn btn-primary">
  <svg width="14" height="14" stroke="currentColor">...</svg> Primary
</button>
<button class="btn btn-success">Success</button>
<button class="btn btn-danger">Danger</button>

<!-- Alternative Styles -->
<button class="btn btn-soft-warning">Soft</button>
<button class="btn btn-outline-info">Outline</button>
<button class="btn btn-ghost-secondary">Ghost</button>

<!-- Sizes: btn-xs, btn-sm, btn-lg, btn-xl -->
<button class="btn btn-primary btn-sm">Small</button>
<button class="btn btn-primary btn-lg">Large</button>

<!-- Shapes -->
<button class="btn btn-primary" style="border-radius: 9999px;">Pill</button>
<button class="btn btn-primary btn-block">Block</button>
States & Effects
💡 Try: Click "Click to Load" button, hover over "Elevate" for shadow effect
HTML 
<!-- States -->
<button class="btn btn-primary">Normal</button>
<button class="btn btn-primary" disabled>Disabled</button>
<button class="btn btn-primary is-loading">Loading</button>

<!-- Icon Only -->
<button class="btn btn-icon btn-primary" title="Edit">
  <svg width="16" height="16" stroke="currentColor">...</svg>
</button>

<!-- Effects -->
<button class="btn btn-primary btn-press-effect">Press</button>
<button class="btn btn-primary btn-elevate">Elevate</button>
Button Groups
HTML 
<!-- Joined Group -->
<div class="btn-group">
  <button class="btn btn-secondary btn-sm">Day</button>
  <button class="btn btn-secondary btn-sm active">Week</button>
  <button class="btn btn-secondary btn-sm">Month</button>
</div>

<!-- Separated Group -->
<div class="btn-group-separated">
  <button class="btn btn-primary">Save</button>
  <button class="btn btn-secondary">Cancel</button>
</div>
